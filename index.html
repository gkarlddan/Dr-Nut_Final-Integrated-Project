Our History

Dr. Nut was a soda from New Orleans first introduced in the 1930s. It was unfortunately discontinued in the 1970s. But now it is back and better than ever! We sought to bring back this beloved drink from ages past but with a new coat of paint. We wanted to keep what made this drink beloved to many during its original run but update it to fit in with the modern aesthetics of a soda brand.

Try a nutty new flavour today with a classic drink!
