Our History

Over the years, Dr. Nut has grown from a simple brand, to going off-sale for a period of time.
During those years, we've been behind the scenes, working together to bring a comeback to the Dr. Nut brand as a whole.

As of today, we're working on new flavours, and improving our brand as a whole.

In our debut, Dr. Nut was a competitive brand. We set our prices high, and our goals higher. Our original ideas were to become a popular, and luxurious drink. As our original goal became unattainable, and the brand went out of business, we changed our direction to be more for the general public.

Now, we look to our patrons, anyone who's interested in our line, and welcome anyone willing to try our products!
